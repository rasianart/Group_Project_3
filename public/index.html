Patrick Segura is a web developer - conceptually driven by untuitive design, modular structures, and alternative modes of

interactivity/connectivity. He is also video, performance, sound, and installation artist living in Chicago. He graduated from

Northwestern University in Computer Science, received his MFA in Art and Technology at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in

2014 and his BFA in Painting from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette in 2011. Dealing with principles of design through research of

the body, play, and virtuality, he draws from his background of gaming to create structures for understanding these ideas and their

relationships. Patrick is a Luminarts Fellow, a Pilotenkueche Resident, as well as a College Art Association Fellow. Patrick has shown

work in the Prospect 2 Biennial, Acadiana Center for the Arts, Sullivan Gallery in Chicago, and Spinnerei Rundgang, Germany.
